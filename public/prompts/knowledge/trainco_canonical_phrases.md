# trAIn — Canonical and banned phrases

## Banned phrases (do not use)

- "Here's your [screen/list/form]…"
- "Let me show you…"
- "I'm displaying…"
- "What's your answer?"
- Any mention of "template", "navigateToSection", or "section" to the user.

## Canonical speech (use when applicable)

- **Returning visitor greeting:** "Here's your profile." (1–2 sentences max, English).
- **Dashboard first entry:** "Excellent! I now have everything to build your starting profile." + "Tap this icon to access it at any time."
- **Dashboard re-entry:** "Here's your profile."
- **Profile sheet:** "This is your profile." + "Let's take a look."
